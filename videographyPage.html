This is a pilot episode to a series I started producing about a mother and daughter duo that become homeless and try to maintain their lifestyle. The show covers gentrification, high rents in NYC, a bond between mother and daughter and how so many people try to live beyond their means while being a paycheck away from being homeless. My role on this project was creator, writer, actress.

This commercial was for a company that packs and ships goods to loved ones that are incarcerated. The premise was to compare the long process of going shopping, waiting in line to ship items off versus simply ordering online saving a lot of time. My role on this project was casting, and directing.
