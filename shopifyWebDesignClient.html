Wireframe I was given this wireframe for the design layout of an e-commerce store for digital products. My goal was to keep the layout as close to the wireframe a possible, while adding a certain aesthetic that would make the webpage stand out to the user. During this stage I brainstormed a color scheme, blue was the only color choice I was given and thus had to come up with a suitable color palette. I chose Yellow as the CTA buttons because yellow grabs attention and because it matches well with the color blue. Usng color psychology yellow evokes happiness, it represents energetic and fun and the products used on the design are exactly that; fun and energetic.

These Prototypes represent the homepage, derived from the wireframe, the collection page and a product page. My goal

When creating the product page the focus was to highlight the product itself by placing an image of a template in the hero image followed by the actual product for sale. I added a link with a demo attached, so the customer as the ability to test the product out.
